Groundwater Status Report: Shirol

Executive Summary

Good news! The groundwater situation in **Shirol** is currently **SAFE**. This means there's plenty of groundwater available, and we are using it responsibly.

Only about **38.6%** of the available groundwater is currently being used, leaving a large surplus for future needs. This healthy balance is thanks to good natural recharge from sources like rainfall and agriculture.

**Based on this data, you can likely dig a borewell in Shirol.**

Key Insights

  • **Groundwater Category:** The region is categorized as **"SAFE"**. This is the best category, indicating that groundwater resources are in good health and can be developed further.
  • **Current Usage (Stage of Extraction):** We are only extracting about **38.6%** of the total available groundwater. This low usage rate is excellent and ensures long-term sustainability.
  • **Abundant Availability:** Shirol has a total groundwater availability of **10,819.52 Million Cubic Meters (MCM)** per year.
  • **Future Potential:** A significant **6,646.66 MCM** is available for future use, showing a healthy reserve even after current extraction.
  • **Strong Recharge:** The groundwater system is well-replenished by natural processes like rainfall and water returning from agricultural activities, contributing to its "safe" status.
